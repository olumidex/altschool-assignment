ABOUT ME

My Story

My name is Olumide (#Omidev), i have always been passionate about technology and design. Ever since i was young, i loved tinkering with computers and creating visually appealing projects. As i grew older, i realized that my interests aligned perfectly with the field of frontend engineering, where i could combine my technical skills with my eye for design to build engaging and user-friendly web applications. Determined to turn my passion into a career, so i began researching educational programs that could provide me with the necessary skills and knowledge. After considering several options, i discovered Altschools. The comprehensive curriculum, experienced instructors, and hands-on learning approach immediately caught my attention..

Reasons for Joinning Altschool

  • Structured Learning Path: Altschools offers a well-structured program that covers the essential technologies and tools
    used in frontend development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks like React and Angular.
  • Practical Experience: The program emphasizes practical, project-based learning. i was excited about the opportunity to build real-world
    projects, which would not only reinforce my learning but also help me to create a portfolio to showcase my skills to potential employers.
  • Mentorship and Support: Altschools provides access to experienced mentors who can offer guidance, answer questions, and provide
    valuable industry insights. i knew that having a mentor would be instrumental in my growth and development as a frontend engineer.
  • Community and Networking: The vibrant community at Altschools allows students to connect with like-minded individuals, share knowledge,
    and collaborate on projects. i was eager to join this community to learn from others and expand my professional network.
  • Flexibility and Accessibility: Altschools offers flexible learning schedules, allowing me to balance my studies with other commitments.
    Additionally, the online format makes it accessible from anywhere, which was particularly important for me.
